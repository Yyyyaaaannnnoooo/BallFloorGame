THIS IS AN AMAZING AND HARD GAME!
USE YOUR THUMB TO MOVE THE BALL LEFT OR RIGHT!
TAP ON THE SCREEN TO MAKE THE BALL JUMP!
if on a laptop use left and right arrows to move and UP ARROW OR MOUSE CLICK TO JUMP!
ONCE YOU JUMPED YOU NEED TO TOUCH ONE OF THE MOVING OBJECTS TO JUMP AGAIN.
HOLD YOUR FINGER / MOUSE PRESSED ON THE SCREEN TO START THE GAME!
